By Plane

The closest airport is Bergerac airport where Ryanair runs a twice weekly service from London. From Bergerac, the Chateau is a half an hour drive away and we would be happy to help you arrange a taxi for you. Alternatively, you can fly into Bordeaux airport which is regularly served by flights from the UK and the rest of Europe. There are also internal fligths from Paris and Lyon. From Bordeaux airport, the Chateau is a 1.5h drive away.

By Car

From Bordeaux city centre, first take the N230 motorway. Take exit 26 onto the N89 motorway heading towards Libourne and follow the signs heading to Lyon. Take exit 9 out of the N89 on the exit towards Castillon. Take the D670 exit towards Castillon. When you arrive at Castillon, continue on the D936 motorway and continue following the signs to Saint-Philippe-du-Seignal. At Saint-Philippe, keep right to follow the Rue des Primeveres for 2.6km. Take the first two rights and you will arrive at the Chateau. The journey time is approximately 1.5h.

By Train

From Bordeaux, take the TER train from Bordeaux Saint-Jean heading towards Bergerac. Alight at Sainte-Foy-la-Grande. From there, the Chateau is a 15min drive away.
